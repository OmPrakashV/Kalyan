About Dr. Kalyan Vangara

Dedicated to Your Journey Through Cancer Care

With over 11 years of specialized experience in surgical oncology, I am committed to providing comprehensive, patient-centered cancer care in Hyderabad. My expertise spans Gastrointestinal, Breast, Gynecologic, Head & Neck, and HPB oncology.

My approach combines open, laparoscopic, and robotic surgical techniques with compassionate care, ensuring each patient receives personalized treatment. Having trained at Kidwai Memorial Institute of Oncology, Bengaluru, I bring experience from high-volume tertiary cancer centers.

I am actively involved in clinical research and academic teaching, with published systematic reviews and meta-analyses in peer-reviewed journals, along with training in Molecular Oncology from RGCC, Greece.
